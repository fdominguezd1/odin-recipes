Bogotan Changua: the egg, bread an milk traditional soup

Changua is a soup from the classic culinary repertoire of Colombia's Cundinamarca-Boyac√° highlands, which could not be easier and more delicious. Its ingredients are few: egg, water, milk, green onion, cilantro, bread, and sometimes white cheese or potatoes. Changua is usually served for breakfast, but it is also an alternative for a light lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • Green onions, chopped
  • 2 cups of milk
  • Salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 toast, chopped in medium size pieces
  • Fresh cilantro, shredded

Preparation

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat the water in an earthenware pot over medium heat.

  2. Step 2

    3. Add the green onion and cook for 2 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add milk and salt. Reduce the temperature and add the eggs.

  4. Step 4

    5. Cook for 2 minutes or until the eggs are cooked.

  5. Step 5

    6. Add the bread pieces over the changua and garnish with the chopped cilantro.

  6. Step 6

    7. Serve and enjoy!
